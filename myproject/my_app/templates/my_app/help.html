Help & User Guide

Everything you need to know about navigating StockFlow.

1 Getting Started

Once you log in, you'll land on the Dashboard. This provides a high-level overview of your total products, items with low stock, and items that are currently out of stock.

2 Managing Products

Use the Add Product button in the sidebar to register new items in your inventory. You can view, update, or delete existing items through the Inventory List page.

3 Stock In & Stock Out

To change quantities of existing products, go to Stock Management:

  • Stock In: Add quantity to a product when you receive new supply.
  • Stock Out: Remove quantity when items are sold or moved.
  • Stock History: Review every movement ever recorded.

4 User Administration

Admins can access User Management to create new team member accounts, assign roles (Admin, Manager, Staff), and monitor their active status.
